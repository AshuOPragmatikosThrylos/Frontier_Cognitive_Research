# Meta Levels

Questions operate at different levels of abstraction. Higher intelligence may correspond to deeper meta-levels.

## Layer 0

Questions about the world.

**Example:** What is consciousness?

## Layer 1

Questions about investigations.

**Example:** How do we investigate consciousness?

## Layer 2

Questions about questions.

**Example:** What makes a good question?

## Layer 3

Questions about reasoning itself.

**Example:** Can questions investigate themselves?

## Depth

Meta-levels stack. Each layer questions the layer below — methods, objects of inquiry, and the reasoning that connects them. Recursive self-improvement may originate when meta-questions drive refinement across levels.
