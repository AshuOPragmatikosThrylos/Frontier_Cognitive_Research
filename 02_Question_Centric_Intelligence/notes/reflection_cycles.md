# Reflection Cycles

Inquiry may loop through observation and reflection. Reflection is hypothesized to be as fundamental as observation.

## Cycle

Observe

↓

Question

↓

Investigate

↓

Answer

↓

Reflect

↓

Question the Questioning Process

↓

Observe

## Reflection

After an answer is formed, the system may examine whether the question was well posed, whether investigation was sound, and what new unknowns emerged. This produces meta-questions and returns attention to observation with refined methods.

## Loop

The cycle does not terminate at answers. It continues through reflection back into the world — and back into questions about how questioning itself works.
