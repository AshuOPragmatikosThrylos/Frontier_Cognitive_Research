# Intelligence and Contradiction

Contradiction appears wherever a model meets the world and fails to align.

## Evolution

Organism != Environment

Organisms that mismatch their environment face pressure to adapt. Survival depends on resolving the tension between internal structure and external conditions.

## Science

Theory != Observation

Theories predict; instruments observe. When they diverge, science advances by questioning and revising — not by ignoring the gap.

## Learning

Prediction != Reality

Learning systems update when predictions fail. Error is not noise alone; it signals contradiction between model and experience.

## Intelligence

Intelligence may be the process of resolving contradictions — detecting mismatch, forming questions, investigating, and revising models until tension is reduced or reframed.
