# Contradiction

Contradiction is the mismatch between expectation and reality.

When what is predicted or assumed conflicts with what is observed, the world model cannot hold both without revision.

## Example

**Expectation:** Birds fly.

**Reality:** Penguins do not.

The contradiction between category and instance breaks naive compression and demands explanation.

## Questions

Questions arise from contradiction. A question names the gap between expectation and reality — and invites investigation to resolve or refine the tension.
