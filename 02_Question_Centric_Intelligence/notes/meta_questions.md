# Meta Questions

Questions can generate meta-questions — questions about how to ask, investigate, or evaluate other questions.

## Example Chain

What is consciousness?

↓

How do we investigate consciousness?

↓

What makes a good question?

↓

Can questions investigate themselves?

Each step turns inquiry inward. Meta-questions do not replace object-level questions; they refine the process that pursues them.

## Role

Meta-questions enable reflection, self-correction, and improvement of the questioning process itself. They may spawn new lineages within the question graph.
