# Terminology

## Observation

A raw input or measurement from the environment, before it is fully explained or absorbed into a world model.

## Compression Failure

When an observation cannot be cleanly encoded into the current world model. The mismatch between what was expected and what was observed.

## Question

A structured representation of something unknown or unresolved. A first-class entity that can be tracked, prioritized, and evolved over time.

## Curiosity Debt

The accumulated pressure from unresolved questions and compression failures. Acts as a drive toward exploration and model revision.

## World Model

An internal representation of how the environment works. Used to predict, explain, and compress observations.
