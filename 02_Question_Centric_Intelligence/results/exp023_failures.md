# EXP-023 Failures

Post-experiment failure analysis for question removal and organization survival.

Date: 2026-06-22  
Experiment: EXP-023 Question Removal

---

## Failure Analysis

**Question removal did not collapse organization.**

World B eliminated question objects, question IDs, question states, memory, reconstruction, and fresh emergence. Expected structural collapse did **not** occur. Six difference groups and three persistent tensions remained — identical to World A at the organizational layers below questions.

This is a **program-level falsification of questions-as-fundamental**, not a mechanical failure. Both pipelines completed deterministically.

## Current Limitation

### Organization still depends upon tensions and differences

World B survival does not imply organization is cost-free or primitive-free. Structure requires:

1. **Difference groups** — behavior splits within categories (6 groups)
2. **Persistent tensions** — paired group conflicts above threshold (3 tensions)

Questions were removed; **tensions and differences were not**. The floor has moved down one layer but has not been reached.

### Question layer may still be useful

World A demonstrates questions as **compression/convenience** — human-readable tension expressions with lifecycle dynamics. Optional, not structural.

## Overall Assessment

EXP-023 **significantly reduced confidence in questions as fundamental entities** and **increased confidence in tensions as deeper organizing principles**.

**Did not achieve:**

- Collapse of organization without questions (hypothesis falsified in reverse)
- Identification of absolute bedrock (tensions/differences remain)
- Renaming the program (name now ironic — documented honestly)

**Did achieve:**

- Questions optional for organization (H187)
- Tensions alone maintain structure (H188)
- Candidate hierarchy: Difference → Tension; above optional
- Third consecutive destructive test with clear survivor layer

**Scientific value:**

One of the most important experiments completed so far. A program called Question-Centric Intelligence that removes questions and finds structure intact is either bankrupt or **honest**. The result supports honesty: questions compress tensions; they do not ground organization.

Next attack: **tensions** — determine whether difference alone suffices, or whether tension is the true floor.
