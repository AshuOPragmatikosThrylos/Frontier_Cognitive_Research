# EXP-022 Failures

Post-experiment failure analysis for persistence removal and organization survival.

Date: 2026-06-22  
Experiment: EXP-022 Persistence Removal

---

## Failure Analysis

**Persistence removal did not collapse organization.**

World B removed stable memory traces, identity preservation, and reconstruction. Expected collapse of organizational coherence did **not** occur. Six difference groups, three persistent tensions, and three live questions survived — identical structural counts to World A.

This is a **program-level falsification of persistence-as-fundamental**, not a mechanical failure. Both pipelines completed deterministically.

---

## Current Limitation

### Questions still depended on tensions and differences

Removing persistence did not reach below the tension layer. Questions in World B reappeared because:

1. Difference groups persisted in `difference_groups` dict
2. Tensions reopened as persistent on reintroduction
3. `emerge_fresh_question()` compressed tension back into live question

The experiment falsified persistence but **did not test** whether organization survives tension removal or difference removal. The floor has not yet been found.

### Memory may still be an engineering optimization

World A reconstruction preserves history and enables identity continuity — useful for narrative coherence even if not structurally necessary. EXP-022 demotes memory from **primitive** to **optimization**.

## Overall Assessment

EXP-022 **significantly reduced confidence in persistence as a fundamental principle** and **increased confidence in deeper organizing mechanisms** (difference, tension, fresh emergence).

**Did not achieve:**

- Collapse of organization without persistence
- Identification of absolute primitive (floor not yet reached)
- Proof that memory is useless (it optimizes continuity in World A)

**Did achieve:**

- Persistence falsified as fundamental (H178)
- Organization survival without memory (H180–H181)
- Fresh emergence as alternative to reconstruction (H182–H183)
- Memory and identity demoted to optional (H184–H185)
- Difference and tension elevated (H186)

**Scientific value:**

One of the most important experiments completed so far. Paired with EXP-021, establishes a **destructive testing methodology**: remove assumptions, observe what survives, repeat on the survivor. Selection destroyed (021); persistence destroyed as fundamental (022); **difference and tension remain**.

Next attack vector: **questions**, then **tensions**, then **differences** — find the true floor or discover that the stack has no single primitive.
