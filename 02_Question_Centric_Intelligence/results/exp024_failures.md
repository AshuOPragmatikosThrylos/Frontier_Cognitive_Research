# EXP-024 Failures

Post-experiment failure analysis for tension removal and organization survival.

Date: 2026-06-22  
Experiment: EXP-024 Tension Removal

---

## Failure Analysis

**Tension removal did not collapse organization.**

World B eliminated tension objects, tension IDs, tension strength, and persistent tensions. Expected structural collapse did **not** occur. Six difference groups remained — identical to World A at the organizational layer below tensions.

This is a **program-level falsification of tensions-as-fundamental**, not a mechanical failure. Both pipelines completed deterministically.

## Current Limitation

### Organization still depends upon differences

World B survival does not imply organization is cost-free or primitive-free. Structure requires:

1. **Difference groups** — behavior splits within categories (6 groups)
2. **Minimum member threshold** — ≥2 entities per behavior group to form a group

Tensions were removed; **differences were not**. The floor has moved down one layer but has not been reached.

### Tension layer may still be useful

World A demonstrates tensions as **pairwise summaries** — explicit conflict labels and strength ranking between groups. Optional, not structural.
