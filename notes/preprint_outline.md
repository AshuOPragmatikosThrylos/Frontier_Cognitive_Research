## Abstract Ideas (bullet sketch)

- Motivation: answer-centric AI vs anomaly-centric cognition.
- Method: sixteen deterministic Python experiments, no LLM/embeddings/randomness.
- Arc: build question ecology → introduce scarcity and evolution → falsify with controls → reduce to difference-first → add lifecycle and distributed memory.
- Key finding (tentative): **organization and memory can persist when questions are absent or extinct**; questions may be compressed episodic representations of persistent tension.
- Limitations: single toy domain, hand-tuned constants, no empirical validation.
- Contribution type: **conceptual framework + reproducible negative results** (hierarchy collapse, random controls, minimal worlds).
